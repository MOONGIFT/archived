**JS\_PSYME.XP - 概　要** 　[http://www.trendmicro.co.jp/vinfo/virusencyclo/default5.asp?VName=JS%5FPSYME%2EXP&VSect=P](http://www.trendmicro.co.jp/vinfo/virusencyclo/default5.asp?VName=JS%5FPSYME%2EXP&VSect=P)
